About the designer

"Good design is nothing you notice — it is everything you feel."

With over 8 years of practice spanning residential, hospitality, and commercial spaces, Ghanashyam brings a deeply intuitive approach to every project. Based in Ernakulam, the work extends across Kerala, India, and internationally — from boutique salons to retail kiosks in Abu Dhabi and Canada.

The studio believes in slow, considered design — taking time to understand how a client truly lives before a single piece of furniture is chosen or a wall is painted.
